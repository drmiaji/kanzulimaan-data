# Privacy Policy for Masayele Qurbani (Islamic Sacrifice Rules)

**Effective Date:** May 21, 2026

## 1. No User Data Collection
This application ("the App") is an educational Islamic reference book covering topics of *Qurbani* (sacrifice), Hadith, *Masayele*, and rulings according to *Ahle Sunnat Wal Jamaat*.  

We do **not** collect, store, share, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. This includes but is not limited to:
- Name, email, phone number, address
- Device ID, IP address, location data
- Contacts, photos, files, or call logs

## 2. No Account Required
The App does not require users to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to access its contents (e.g., sacrifice rules, Hadith, fatwas).

## 3. No Permissions Required
The App does not request or utilize any system permissions (e.g., camera, microphone, storage, location). All book content is bundled locally within the App.

## 4. No Third-Party Data Sharing
We do not integrate any analytics, advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob, Facebook Analytics), or crash reporting tools that might collect data. Consequently, no user data is shared with any third party.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). Since no data collection occurs, no parental consent is needed.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by the “Effective Date” above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
